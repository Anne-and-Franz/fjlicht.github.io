About

I have studied a mix of biology, ecology, computational biology, and plant pathology.
Alumnus of Pennsylvania State University, I worked in human pathology at [link to WRAIR!](http://www.wrair.army.mil) I now work on agricultural plant pathogens, particularily fungal pathogens.
I began my graduate studies at the University of New Hampshire in Plant Biology before moving with my lab to CSU.
I have traveled to Senegal in Western Africa for a National Science Foundation fellowship to study siol microbial ecology. And now live in Fort Collins, Colorado.
With my personal time I get outside, work on my current research interests and activities.
